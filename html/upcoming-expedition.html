Markha Valley Trek   < Back
   
Duration 8 days
Best Time June to October
Highest Point Kongmaru La (5135m)
Highlight People, Village, Wildlife and High Passes.
Physical Challenge Easy

........................................................

This trek has been, one of the favourite among trekkers for a long time because of Beautiful Valley of Markha overshadowed by Kanyatse peak and other groups of Peaks. The numerous small villages, shepherd camps and passes are the main feature of this trek. The trek begins from Spituk monastery or Stok village passing through Stok La and Gandala Pass, which takes you across Markha valley. At last it crosses Kongmaru Pass and finishes wonderful trek at the famous Hemis monastery.
