Expeditions: Lamayuru to Darcha Trek Back
   
Duration 19 days
Best Time June to October
Highest Point Shinkhul La (5080m)
Highlight People, Village, Wildlife and High Passes.
Physical Challenge Moderate

........................................................

This is the most popular Trail into Zangskar via Shingo La. This trek starts from famous monastery of Lamayuru and enters Zangskar though most isolated villages of Lingshet, Photoksar and then the headquarters of Zangskar called Padum. Further this trek leads you to Darcha in Himachal Paradesh via Phuktal and Bardan monasteries.

 

The cost includes:-

  • Trekking equipments (sleeping bags, mattress, two men tents, kitchen tent and toilet tents)
  • Camping and wild life fee
  • Guide, cook and helpers
  • Horses for the trek.
  • Transportation(to and from the trekking point)

 Note: The cost does not include:-

  • Mineral water
  • Soft drinks
  • Alcohol
